The report of falls detection, temperature, and heart rate of John Doe has been made by the students of ESIR and EUETIB of biomedical engineering under the supervision of Alfredo Hernandez and Di Ge from the LTSI, University of Rennes 1.

The data used in this program are provided by a Texas Instrument SensorTag (falls and temperature) and by a Polaris belt (heartrate).
The SensorTag part is using a Javascript library by samdeepmistry found on GitHub. The Polaris part is using a selfmade Arduino-Zigbee duo to send data to the computer.
